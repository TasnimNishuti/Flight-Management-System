Drogo Airlines offers premium Ground Handling services for all airlines flying to any location within Bangladesh.

Drogo Airlines provides Ground Handling facilities to all the Airports in Bangladesh. It is approved by the regulatory body, Civil Aviation of Bangladesh, as the sole Ground Handler in Bangladesh to handle all foreign carriers flights operating on commercial basis. It has sufficient ground handling equipments and experienced manpower to handle all type of aircraft.
